Project Overview

Computer-aided drug design & discovery

In this project, I developed an algorithm named XGBRegressor to predict the IC50 of compounds against the enzyme HMG-CoA reductase. HMG-CoA reductase is a critical enzyme in the mevalonate pathway, responsible for cholesterol synthesis in the liver. Inhibiting this enzyme can effectively lower cholesterol levels and prevent diseases related to hypercholesterolemia.

The XGBRegressor algorithm was specifically designed to predict the half-maximal inhibitory concentration (IC50) of various compounds, providing valuable insights into their potency as inhibitors of HMG-CoA reductase. By accurately predicting IC50 values, this algorithm aids in the identification and discovery of new compounds that can serve as potent inhibitors, potentially leading to the development of new drugs to treat diseases associated with high cholesterol levels.
